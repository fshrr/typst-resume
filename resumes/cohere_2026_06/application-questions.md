# Cohere Application Questions

## Q: Tell us about your experience in client-facing roles and/or your experience with AI or agentic systems

Most of my experience has been working directly with people to scope ideas and build useful products across very different settings. As a freelance AI engineer, I've worked with small businesses in industries including immigration consultancy and physiotherapy, using LLM pipelines to build more efficient workflows and increase productivity. With Surgical Safety Technologies, I worked with non-technical healthcare experts to build solutions that merge technology and healthcare to improve surgical quality. With Echoes, I worked closely with a grassroots event community in Toronto, running discovery to understand real problems and build a platform.

On the technical side, my best examples come from freelancing. I built a RAG chatbot for an immigration consultancy that cut case prep from two hours to about twenty minutes, and a physiotherapy app that turns patient voice logs into structured clinical data, cutting session time from 45 to 30 minutes. I also have 5+ years of experience building products and infrastructure around machine learning models.
