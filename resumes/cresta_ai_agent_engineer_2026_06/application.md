# Cresta — AI Agent Engineer — Application Answers

## Q1: Customer problem → technical solution

> Tell us about a time when you worked directly with a customer to understand a problem and developed or deployed a technical solution. What was the problem, What tools or frameworks did you use and what was the impact?

At Echoes Events, hosts told us that many newcomers avoided events because they were reluctant to go to a new event alone. To solve this, we built vector embeddings over user profiles and used cosine similarity to surface "people you might know" on each event page, suggesting attendees they may have crossed paths with at previous events, giving them a familiar face before showing up.

## Q2: Cross-functional collaboration

> Describe a situation where you had to collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver a product or solution. How did you ensure alignment and drive success?

At Surgical Safety Technologies, I sat between ML scientists and surgeons to build a surgical video AI platform, translating technical details for surgeons and relaying medically relevant context back to ML scientists so models produced meaningful analysis. To ensure alignment, I introduced bi-weekly feature demos before development for early clinical feedback, and implemented feature flags so users could compare model and feature versions themselves.
